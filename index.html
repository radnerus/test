Who We Are

We are a group of educational enthusiasts who have come together to develop a set of module that will develop the cognitive skill for students of all ages.

ed-trix is an educational platform which mainly targets in identifying one’s aptitude and helps them to develop their ability in other words ‘Knowledge and Skills’. Our vision is to raise the standard of student’s cognitive functions which will, in turn, enhances one’s intellect.

What We Do

We have everything a student needs to be successfull in his/her life.

Kid-O-Math

Age: 5 years to 14 years

Kid-O-Math is a mathematics development program which is designed for the kids of the ages 5 to 14 years. This program's motto is to make the mathematics easy by teaching the kids how to solve it logically and analytically through a practical approach.

Life Mantra

Age: 5 years to 14 years

Life Mantra is a life-changing platform for the kids of age 5 to 14 years. The students will be introduced to different sociability skills and other cognitive development exercises. This curriculum is constructed through intense search done by our R and D team.

Prime Placement Program

Age: 16 years to 25 years

Prime Placement Program is one of the major programs by ed-trix for college and university students which concentrates on their placement and career skills. ed-trix assures that the students will be equipped with all the skills that one would require to be placed in a MNC.

ACE - Aptitude for Competative Exams

Age: 16 years to 25 years

Ace is the program which has laid the curriculum that has all the effective logical, reasoning, quantitative and verbal aptitude syllabus. This program is for all the people who are preparing for the competative examinations such as CLAT(Common Law Admission Test), MBA entrace exams, bank exams and other goverment exams.

Next Steps

Age: 5 years to 16 years

Next Steps is an English development program which mainly concentrates on the communication skills and other softs kill development which will provide an upper hand for the students and working professional over their peers. Also, this program is designed to be as a fastrack course to make an instant impact within oneself.

Age: 5 years to 18 years

Hack-Lab is a platform formed by ed-trix for the students to learn and improve their knowledge and skills from professionals and experts which will boost their innovative intelligence.
